Getting started with Katie inside your Slack channels

Thank you for adding Katie to your Slack workspace!

In order to use Katie in one of your Slack channels, please do the following steps:

  • Please launch the Slack workspace to which you have added Katie.
  • Select a channel and type "@katie Hello Katie!" in order to add Katie to the selected channel
  • After confirming that Katie should be added to the channel, ask an arbitrary question, such as for example "Who is Katie?"
  • Also have a look at our general Getting started with Katie guide.
