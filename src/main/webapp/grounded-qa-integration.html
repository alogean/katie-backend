Integration of Cohere's Grounded QA

  • Source Code:
    • https://github.com/wyona/sandbox-grounded-qa
    • https://github.com/cohere-ai/sandbox-grounded-qa
  • Katie Grounded QA Connector: https://connector-grounded-qa.katie.qa/swagger-ui/
    • Bearer token required, which was generated by Katie and contains the claim iss="https://katie.qa"
  • Domain Configuration:
    • Click on Settings, e.g. https://app.katie.qa/#/domain/6fdc239c-1e58-4df6-ba57-d841f38a2918/settings
    • Click on Reindex
    • Select Query service and enter the URL: https://connector-grounded-qa.katie.qa/api/v2
