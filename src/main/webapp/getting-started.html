Getting started with Katie

Thank you for using Katie!

To get started please add some QnAs (Questions and Answers) to your knowledge base:

  • Login to the Katie backend.
  • Select one of your domains / knowledge bases.
  • Click on the tab "Questions and Answers" and add a few QnAs by clicking on the button "Add QnA".
  • Click on the tab "Ask Katie", enter a question and see how Katie will answer your question.
