MyKatie helps you to remember whatever you share with her

MyKatie helps you to remember ...

  • appointments
  • interesting articles
  • passwords / credentials
  • contact information
  • shopping list items
  • recipes
  • dates of birth
  • beautiful travel destinations
  • general ideas

... whatever you share with her.

With MyKatie you can get things off your mind. Whatever you would like to share with MyKatie, she will memorize for you and she will help you to remember when you will ask her in the future.

No more multiple apps necessary to remember things.

Examples

Share information with MyKatie like you would share with someone you trust.

Please find below some examples of information you might want to share with Katie to remember:

  • Katie has birthday on July 18
  • We should buy soya sauce, sugar, eggs and oil
  • The island Lefkada in Greece would be great for going on holidays
  • My Netflix password is 123
  • The best seats in the cinema Alba are seats 12 and 13, row 5
  • The research paper https://www.researchgate.net/publication/343618564_KBot_A_Knowledge_Graph_Based_ChatBot_for_Natural_Language_Understanding_Over_Linked_Data is very interesting
  • The phone number of my health insurance is +41 44 404 84 84
  • Here is a great recipe for english scones: Ingredients .... Preparation ...

Katie will help you to remember whatever you shared with her. You can ask Katie for example:

  • When is the birthday of Katie?
  • What did we want to buy today?
  • What is the name of the island in Greece?
  • What is my Netflix password?
  • Which are the best seats in the movie theatre Alba?
  • Which paper is about knowledge graphs?
  • What is the phone number of my health insurance?
  • What is the recipe to make english scones?
