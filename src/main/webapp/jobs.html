  • Data Scientist

    About the role

    The Data Scientist role is responsible for building NLP / NLU systems that can perform previously impossible tasks or achieve unprecedented levels of performance. This requires good engineering (for example designing, implementing, and improving a massive-scale distributed machine learning system), writing high quality machine learning code, and building the science behind the algorithms employed.

    We expect you to

    • Have strong experience in Data Science & Machine Learning and be comfortable with closely collaborating with other teams such as engineering and product management.
    • Fetch information from various sources and generate value for better understanding the data to non-technical and technical stakeholders
    • Generate ML models based on such data and develop or select the appropriate and most efficient algorithm for the given problem
    • Apply understanding of advanced machine learning practices to enhance product performance.
    • Translate business questions into actionable research
    • Know data mining techniques in one or more areas of statistical modeling methods, time series, text mining, optimization, and information retrieval
    • Have the ability to produce workflows using classification, clustering, regression, and dimensionality reduction
    • Have strong communication skills and value building strong relationships with colleagues and partners as well as the ability to explain sophisticated topics in straightforward terms

    Nice to have

    • 5+ years of hands-on technical experience in a data science role
    • Proficient in data handling suites PYTHON, Spark, R, HIVE, SAS, SQL, or similar packages
    • Bachelor's/MS/Ph.D. in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or other quantitative fields
